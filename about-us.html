 MEHDI MOHAMMADI COMMERCIAL BROKERGE is a wholesale Steel Company situated in steel company in UAE. We distribute a wide variety of general trading steel products in the wholesale steel companies of Dubai. We have developed a reputation as the region's specialist in wholesale import and export of steel products to clients all over the region (GCC). Our portfolio boasts over different kinds of steel product lines, not limited to steel equipment but ranging across products such as:


• Providing the best quality steel products and to be competitive in global markets.

• Helping self-sufficiency of country and the regions, by implementing R&D program.

• Developing science and increasing level of theoretical and practical knowledge of our     personnel by continuous training in the    company.

• Products’ quality assurance in compliance with the world standards to establish competitive place in the global markets.

• To trade steel products for a sustainable future.


Achievement of our goals, is possible by continuous innovation, optimum allocation of the resources, environmentally adaptable and friendly methods for steel production, application of best ethical practices and standards in the industry, recruitment of the most capable workforce and continuously investing on our human capital resources in various aspects, and maximizing return on the investment. This company has acquired loyal customers in the form of wholesalers and retailers as well as consumers across UAE and other regions. We have strong brand partners across the globe owing to our excellent dealing service and trading experience. The diverse portfolio of steel products that we have accumulated under one roof keeps growing in number and so does our capacity to satisfy the wholesale, retail, and consumer needs of the steel trading market.
